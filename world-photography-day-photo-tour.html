World Photography Day – Photo Tour

World Photography Day – 19 th August. Lightdots celebrated this day in 2017 with a fabulous photo tour. An amazing group consisting of members who completed our basic photography courses started out with a single objective .i.e. to honour this day by taking breathtaking  pictures. We  covered Madhu Giri fort, which enabled everyone to capture the combination of stonescape, landscape,and cloudscape all together. The trekking experience added more challenge to the photographer in us. After visiting this great place ,our next spot was Namada Chilume. A place with lots of history, picturesqueness and some animal habitat. The drizzling rain brought out  a sparkle in our images. The last spot which we covered on this awesome day was DD Hills.

We could see a marvellous blend of nature, when we reached there by dusk. The landscape with rain approaching us. The beautiful weather made us embrace the feast before us with camera and awe. I thank all the alumnus for making the day very special. It was a great learning and get-together for all of us. (Special thanks to Mr. Harsha and Mr Pramod for planning & organising)
